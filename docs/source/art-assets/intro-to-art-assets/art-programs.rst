Medibang
~~~~~~~~

| Type: Digital painting and Comic creation software
| Price: Free. Paid for extra features.

Great for the basics. 

Advantage:
^^^^^^^^^^

- Easier to get into, simpler interface.
- Still is entirely usable as a free program.
- Can also be used on mobile tablets.

Disadvantage: 
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

- Lacking in filters.
- Brush does not support much settings for customization.
